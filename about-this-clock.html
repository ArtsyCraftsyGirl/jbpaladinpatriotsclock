ABOUT THIS CLOCK

The "JB PALADIN PATRIOTS CLOCK" is a truly unique and revolutionary way of telling time. Our clock’s innovative design will amaze the world in the exciting way that people will perceive and tell time.This is not just a typical or traditional clock; our groundbreaking clock uses colorful lights to indicate the time instead of the traditional hands or digital numbers found on ordinary clocks.This makes our clock extraordinary, ensuring it stands out as unique and special among the myriad of clocks in the world today.

The "JB PALADIN PATRIOTS CLOCK" stands as a heartfelt tribute to our enduring love for America, celebrating the courageous men and women who have selflessly dedicated themselves to serving our nation, both past and present. At "JB Paladin," we take immense pride in designing clocks that reflect not just the passage of time but also capture the spirit of patriotism that binds us as a nation in a way that no other clock can. The name "Patriots Clock" embodies our commitment to honoring the values and freedoms that define our great country, the United States of America.By integrating our brand name,"JB Paladin," we add a personal touch that emphasizes our identity and dedication to craftsmanship.

In celebration of the Semiquincentennial (250th) birthday of the United States of America on July 4, 2026, we proudly introduce the "JB PALADIN PATRIOTS CLOCK." This stunning clock draws inspiration from the bold colors and iconic design of the American flag, capturing the spirit of America's Declaration of Independence in 1776. In addition to the 12 stars located at the hour positions, a radiant gold-colored 13th star at the very center of the clock symbolizes the original 13 colonies of the United States of America and honors the brave individuals who have paid the ultimate price for defending our country.

The star that denotes the correct hour of time is dark blue, which stands for those who are currently serving on active duty, while the light blue five-minute star pays tribute to all who have served in the past. We chose a color inspired by the iconic Statue of Liberty for the clock's face, adding a meaningful touch that reinforces our connection to freedom and hope.The creative genius behind this remarkable clock is none other than Mr. Jeff Birge, whose vision shines through in every intricate detail. After years of anticipation, we are thrilled to see his dream come to life, with the help of his programmer, Ehllen Quezada, who collaborated with Jeff on this project.

Ehllen’s skill in programming languages such as HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript brought this clock to fruition. Additionally,Ehllen’s extensive knowledge in Information Technology played a crucial role in transforming Jeff’s ideas and concepts into a vibrant,fully functional app, which will now be available for all electronic devices. There is no doubt that the "JB PALADIN PATRIOTS CLOCK" is bound to inspire admiration with its exclusive design and unique functions. The moment has finally arrived; we know every American will love this magnificent clock as it pays homage to the greatest country that the world has ever seen: the United States of America!
